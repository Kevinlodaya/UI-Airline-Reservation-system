Non-Smoking Flights ->The Government of India prohibits smoking on all Anant Airlines flights. As a separate precautionary measure, all toilets are fitted with smoke detectors. Reservation ->We suggest that you make your reservations as far in advance as possible through any Anant Airlines office or approved Travel Agent. You can also book pay and purchase your e-ticket using our online booking facility. We kindly request you to supply a contact number during reservation for each city in your itinerary. This will enable us to inform you of any irregularity with a flight on which you are booked.
